# Adai 

## Ingredients 

### Grind:

- [ ] 1 cup rice 
- [ ] 1/6 cup toor dal 
- [ ] 1/6 cup bengal gram dal (channa dal, not puttu kadale)
- [ ] 1/6 cup udad dal 
- [ ] 3 red chillies 
- [ ] 2 green chillies 
- [ ] Coconut 


### Other 

- [ ] Curry leaves
- [ ] A small amount of onion
- [ ] Coriander leaves 
- [ ] Turmeric  

## Steps

1. Soak all ingredients together for 2-4 hours 
2. Grind to a coarse paste
3. Add other ingredients 
4. Cook like a dose
